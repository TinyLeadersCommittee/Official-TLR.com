Banlist & Watchlist

The Tiny Leaders: Reborn ban list is divided into two categories: cards banned in the 50-card deck and cards banned only as a Tiny Leader (commander). It also utilizes a watchlist.

Schedule: Regularly scheduled ban and watchlist announcements occur on the Monday following the release date of each Standard-legal set.

Latest Update: November 25, 2025

Avatar: The Last Airbender — Post-Release Update

  • Underworld Breach: Remains on Watchlist. Observation continues.
  • Edric, Spymaster of Trest: Unbanned (Legal in 50) & moved to Watchlist.
  • Metalworker: Unbanned (Legal in 50) & moved to Watchlist.

For the full reasoning regarding these shifts, read the Full Announcement.

Categorically Excluded Cards

  • Silver-bordered, Gold-bordered, or Acorn-stamped cards
  • Cards with the card type Conspiracy
  • Racially or culturally offensive cards
  • Cards that reference playing for ante
  • Cards that bring stickers or attractions into the game
