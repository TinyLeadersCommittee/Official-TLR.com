## Section 100: Game Concepts

**100.2e:** TL:R decks are subject to additional deckbuilding restrictions and requirements. See rule 906.

**100.4e:** In TL:R, sideboards may consist of no more than 10 cards. The singleton limit applies to the combined deck and sideboard.

**100.5f:** There is no maximum deck size for non-Commander and non-TL:R decks.

**103.2c:** In a Commander or TL:R game, each player puts their commander(s) from their deck face up in the command zone.
