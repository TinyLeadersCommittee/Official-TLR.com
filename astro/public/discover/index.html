Discover TLR

Tiny Leaders: Reborn is a fast, competitive 1v1 Magic format, directly derived and developed from the Original Tiny Leaders format. With 50-card singleton decks and a strict 3-mana-value cap, TLR compresses deep strategy into tight, expressive games. Designed for players who enjoy focused deckbuilding and interactive play.
